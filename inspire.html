What makes a hero?

A hero is someone who demonstrates courage, selflessness, integrity, and perseverance, often inspiring others through their actions and making a positive impact on the world.

Goku has many of these heroic qualities. He shows courage, consistently faces evil opponents and risks his life to protect his loved ones and Earth. He also demostrates selflessness by prioritizing the safety of others over his own. Through his adventures, Goku inspires others with his dedication to training, his huge optimism and his belief in the power of friendship and teamwork.
