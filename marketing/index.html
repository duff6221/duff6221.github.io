About the University of Northern Colorado

Located in Greeley, Colorado, UNC is a public university offering more than 200 programs. Known for education, performing arts and health sciences programs, UNC provides students with excellent professional opportunities.

Learn more at unco.edu.

Why We Support UNC

  • Affordable and high quality education

Academics at UNC

UNC offers over 200 programs, including nationally recognized degrees in education, business, health sciences, and art.

Research & Innovation

UNC faculty and students engage in impactful research across many fields, from education to health sciences. The university offers a culture, providing students with opportunities to contribute to solving real world problems.

Campus Life at UNC

UNC's Greeley campus offers a great student life, with over 150 clubs and organizations, sports teams, and performance arts opportunities. Students enjoy a community in a great setting, with access to a range of student services and support.

Admissions to UNC

Applying to UNC is straightforward. The university offers both undergraduate and graduate programs, with clear admission requirements and financial aid options. Visit the admissions page for more details.
